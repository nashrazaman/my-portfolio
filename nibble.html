NIBBLE
Nibble is an app targeted for college students who
have tight budgets for food! As a college student
I have often struggled to decide what to cook for dinner
with the groceries I have at home. With Nibble, your
virtual pantry, you can generate recipes with ingredients
available at home! To update the app, you could either
manually add your ingredients or take a picture of your
receipt from the grocery store, and the app will
automatically add it to your pantry.
Nibble was my capstone project, my team and I used
python flask for the backend and react native for the
front end. I was the project manager and front end lead
for this project and was responsible for coming up with
the UI/UX of the app and building the front end of the app.
I also helped assist in building the API points and testing!
While the app remains unfinished, we managed
to finish our MVP (minimum viable product) for the app.
